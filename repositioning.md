## 1. New section — "A Different Kind of Creator Network"

**Placement**: Right after Hero, before "What Makes Us Different".
**Purpose**: Frames the category before the buyer reads anything else.

### Section tag
`Our approach`

### Headline
We're not a UGC marketplace.
We're a community that also makes UGC.

### Sub-text
UGC marketplaces solved sourcing — they connect brands with thousands of creators online, and they do it well. We do a different job. We build creator communities on college campuses across India, and the content comes out of that naturally.

### Two-card split

**Card 1 — UGC Marketplaces**
*Label: The category today*

Source a creator. Get a file. Scale content volume across a global pool.

Great for: high-volume testimonial videos from strangers, product shots at scale, fast remote UGC.

**Card 2 — Faceless**
*Label: What we add*

Build a community. Put your product in their hands — together, in person. Get content, live activations, and community amplification as one campaign.

Great for: product launches, local market entry, live experiences, and campaigns where the story matters as much as the clip.

### Closing line
Both matter. If what you need is fifty testimonial videos, use a marketplace. If what you need is a launch — real people, real rooms, real reach — that's us.

## 2. Updated "What Makes Us Different" section (Web2 tab)

**Placement**: Replace the existing Web2 cards in `Sections.tsx`.
**Purpose**: Make 2 of 6 cards explicitly marketplace-aware.

### Section intro
Content is the output. Community is how we make it.

Most UGC happens remotely — a product gets shipped, a creator films alone, the file gets uploaded. It works. But it's missing the part that makes content feel real: the moment people actually experience your product together. That's the part we build around.

### 6 Cards

**1. A community, not a roster**
Every creator in our network is someone we know. Students in the same campus clubs, showing up every week, supporting each other. Your campaign lands in a room — not in an inbox.

**2. Content comes from real experience**
Our creators use your product together before any camera turns on. They taste the coffee, play the game, try the app — and then they shoot. The content feels different because the experience was real.

**3. Volume no single influencer can match**
Twenty creators, twenty takes, twenty audiences. One campaign. That's the same spend as one mid-tier influencer, with a hundred times the surface area.

**4. Live activations, not just content**
We can bring your product into a room — a college event, a local launch, a product demo — shoot it, and amplify it online, all from one campaign. That's not content delivery. That's field marketing with a camera.

**5. Hyper-local reach**
Our students ARE your local audience. Their friends, their classmates, their followers — all in your city. A Coimbatore cafe gets reels from Coimbatore students. Their followers are your next customers.

**6. Every result verified on-chain**
Campaign data, creator activity, engagement — all recorded on Solana. Not a dashboard screenshot. Permanent, auditable proof that the work happened.
